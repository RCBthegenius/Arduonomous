For Ardu Rover V1.3.3

ALL I/O PINS ARE DIGITAL PINS!

RX0   Nc
TX1   Nc
D2    Nc
D3    Bluetooth IN
D4    Bluetooth OUT
D5  ~ Left Motor A
D6  ~ Left Motor B
D7    IR Input
D8    Nc (This is the Reset pin, but it causes a premature reset everytime the board boots, hence why it isn't plugged in, but if you can get it to work, let me know :)
D9  ~ Right Motor B
D10 ~ Right Motor A
D11   Right Motor Enable
D12   Left Motor Enable
D13   Nc

~ =PWM Pin
Nc =No Connection
